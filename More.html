Did you know that Superman has been played by over 15 actors over the years, each bringing their own twist to the Man of Steel? Here's a look at everyone who's donned the cape on the big screen.

  1. In the first on screen debut of Superman, Kirk Alyn dons the cape, putting reality, for the first time, to the Superman comics (1948-1950)
  2. After multiple Tv Series Releases, Christopher Reeves dons the cape, becoming many peoples definitive Superman (1978-1987)
  3. With every good movie, there are multiple reprises of the plot as decades pass and people begin to yearn for a new Superman. To fulfill this demand was Brandon Routh.
  4. In modern day Superman history, many would argue Henry Cavill is the most iconic actor to don the cape.
  5. In the most recent Superman movie, James Gunn teams with David Corenswet to remix the traditional Superman comics and place a modern flair on the Superman franchise.
